Rosario

In Rosario participants will stay at Ariston Hotel.

Speakers can make their booking at the same hotel. Alternatively, they can contact Ariel Lombardi. In case of dealing directly with the hotel, please remember to mention that you participate in the CIMPA school in order to get corporate fees. Also, be sure to mention that you are foreigners, as paying with a foreign credit card means you will not be charged VAT (IVA)

Buenos Aires

In Buenos Aires participants will stay at Hotel Las Familias.

Regretfully we cannot offer corporate fees in Buenos Aires. We suggest the following hotels:

  • Pampa Plaza Hotel: 5 minutes walk from the Faculty.
  • LM Hotel: more expensive. 10 minutes from the Faculty.

Another good options frecuently used by visitors (all of them around 25 minutes from the Faulty) are:

  • Hotel Ker
  • Hotel Sarum
  • Hotel Pedraza

In all cases, be sure to mention that you are foreigners, as paying with a foreign credit card means you will not be charged VAT (IVA).
