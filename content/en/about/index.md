 The goal of the school is to provide students with a broad overview of the central topicos in numerical analysis and PDEs, from elementary to advanced finite element techniques, and to cover some applications.

There will be nine courses in total, four in the first week (Rosario), and five in the second week (Buenos Aires). Each course will include 4 lectures of 1 hour and 30 minutes each.
