# CIMPA School
### I FCEIA School of Mathematics
#### Rosario - July, 20-24,  2026
### XIV Luis Santaló Winter School
#### Buenos Aires - July, 27-31, 2026


The goal of the school is to provide students with a broad overview of central topics
in numerical analysis of PDEs, from elementary to advanced finite element
techniques, and to cover some applications.

There will be eight courses in total. Four of them during the first week (Rosario) and four on the second week (Buenos Aires). The first week is devoted to those topics that can be regarded more general and introductory while those of the second week are more
advanced. Each course will include 4 lectures, each lasting 1 hour and 30 minutes.
